FeelsApp is a diary and emotions logging app. It lets you create simple notes and associate them with emotions.
The notes offers full encryption with SQLite database. The app was designed for quick usability. The notes are sorted based on date.

The app not only lets you create notes, but also is capable of showing statistics of these notes.

*Encryption
*Ability to see statistics of the notes
*Ability to Encrypt the notes with or without a password
*Colour coding for different emotions and feelings
*Ability to rate your emotions from 2 to 10
